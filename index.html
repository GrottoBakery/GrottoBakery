About Us

Our Story

Grotto Bakery was created to serve a growing community of people who value wellness without sacrificing the joy of eating. What started as a personal journey for healthier eating became a community movement for gluten-free living in Bahrain.

From humble beginnings, Grotto has grown into a dedicated gluten-free bakery committed to transparency, taste, and trust. Every loaf of bread, every sweet treat, and every custom recipe is carefully crafted with handpicked ingredients all free from gluten, preservatives, and fillers.

We believe that food brings people together. That’s why our kitchen is built around a promise: to make delicious gluten-free baked goods that everyone at the table can enjoy whether out of need, choice, or curiosity.

Our team is made up of passionate bakers, nutrition advocates, and food lovers who work every day to bring innovation and love into every product. We're not just a bakery we're a lifestyle choice for better, balanced living.

What is Celiac Disease?

At the heart of the gluten-free community are those who suffer from Celiac disease, a serious genetic autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. It is estimated to affect 1 in 100 people worldwide,We formulated a completely gluten-free products so that those with gluten sensitivities could enjoy our products without worry of cross-contamination.

A low-gluten diet
reduces lactose tolerance

People who suffer from celiac disease or gluten intolerance often exhibit symptoms of lactose intolerance as well. This is because the lining of the gut produces the enzyme lactase which breaks down the lactose found in dairy products. People with a gluten intolerance suffer from damage to the gut which impairs lactase production. However, this effect is temporary, and a gluten-free diet will promote gut health and reduce or even eliminate the symptoms of lactose intolerance.
